What is AI?

AI, or Artificial Intelligence, is a type of technology that allows machines or computers to think, learn, and make decisions like humans. It is used to solve problems, do tasks faster, and make life easier.

AI is now part of our everyday lives and helps in many simple ways. It makes things easier, like giving directions on Google Maps, suggesting movies on Netflix, or letting Chatgpt and Deepseek answer our questions. It can even help with online shopping and keeping our monet safe in banks. However, using AI can also be wrong. It can show us wrong directions or showing suggestions we don't like. It can also collect too much personal information, which may risk our privacy. Some people may depend on it too much and forget to think for themselves. So while AI is very helpful, it also has downsides we need to be careful about.
