About This Topic

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is an advanced technology that enables machines to simulate human thinking and learning. It is widely used to improve daily life by providing quick solutions, personalized suggestions, and efficient support in various tasks.

AI is socially relevant because it helps us in learning, work, health, and communication, making life easier and more connected. However, it also brings concerns like privacy and job loss, so it must be used wisely.
